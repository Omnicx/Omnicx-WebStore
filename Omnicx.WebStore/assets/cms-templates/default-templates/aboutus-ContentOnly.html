Our History

The OmniCX was founded in 2015 as The OmniCX Group’s flagship website. We are a part of The OmniCX Group, which is one of Europe’s largest online retailers. Some of our sister websites include Myprotein, Zavvi, Coggles and Lookfantastic. At The OmniCX we select the finest products from the rest of our group, and use the specialist knowledge from our other sites to help influence our luxury online department store. Our home is in the North-West of England, and from this base we connect with our customers all over the world.
